About the Startup

AI-powered elder care companion named CarePal. Monitors health metrics, medication schedules, and daily routines for seniors. Provides reminders, emergency alerts, and wellness tips. Integrates with wearables and smart home devices for real-time health monitoring. Target audience: elderly individuals and their families, aged 60+, looking for smart healthcare assistance.
